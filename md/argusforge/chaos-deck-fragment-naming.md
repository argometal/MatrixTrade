# Chaos Deck · Fragment (approved naming)

**Status:** Approved by product owner (2026-07-24). Supersedes any earlier draft that used Θήκη/Theke or “Dump”.

## Canonical names

| Role | Name | Notes |
|------|------|--------|
| Binder / container | **Chaos Deck** | What the user opens and works inside. Unchanged. |
| Individual receptacle | **Fragment** | One unit of chaos material inside a Chaos Deck. |

A Chaos Deck is full of fragments.

## Do not use

- Θήκη / Theke / Thecae
- Dump / dumps (as the unit name)
- Generic “note” as the product label for these units (engine type may still be `note` under the hood until a later migration)

## UI copy

- Create: “New Chaos Deck”, “New Fragment”
- Empty state: “No fragments yet”
- Capture: “Save Fragment”, “Add Fragment”
- Move/rename: “Move fragment…”, “Rename fragment”
