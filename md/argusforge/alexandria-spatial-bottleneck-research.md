# Alexandria Research Note — Spatial construction (deferred)

**Status:** Research — **deferred, non-binding**  
**Date:** 2026-07-23  
**Binding freeze:** [`alexandria-frozen-contract.md`](alexandria-frozen-contract.md)  
**AF Phase 0:** [`phase-0-architecture.md`](phase-0-architecture.md)

This note is **historical / exploratory**. It is **not** ArgusForge architecture. It does **not** select a future solution. It does **not** reopen Alexandria.

---

## Problem status

The spatial problem for Alexandria is **not fully formulated**.

Past experiments (environments comparable to Roblox, Google Maps, and similar) suggested that **navigation** was less painful than **constructing** maintainable spatial environments by hand (manual placement, object-by-object Parcours, reconstruction from references).

That observation remains useful history. It is **not** a closed diagnosis and **not** a mandate for any pipeline.

---

## What is intentionally undecided

Until Alexandria is reopened under a dedicated study phase:

- no render pipeline;
- no spatial AI architecture;
- no environment-reconstruction design;
- no 3D engine choice;
- no Realm / Parcour / Room / slot contracts for AF;
- no binding hypothesis that “AI generates spatial representation” or any other specific approach.

Any earlier wording that sounded like a selected direction is **withdrawn as architecture**. Ideas may be revisited later as research questions only.

---

## Cognitive mechanics vs presentation

Alexandria’s cognitive / structural mechanics (Locus, Parcour, Objects, evaluation, scheduling, etc.) are **preserved under freeze** for later inventory — not affirmed as the final product shape.

How knowledge will eventually be **presented spatially** remains an open research area after reopen.

---

## Implication for ArgusForge

AF must not depend on Alexandria.  
Alexandria must not block AF.  
AF Phase 0 does not implement or design Alexandria exchange details.
