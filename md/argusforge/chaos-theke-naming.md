# Chaos naming — Θήκη (Theke) + Dump

## Assignment

| Concept | Term | Note |
|--------|------|------|
| Binder of poured capture | **Θήκη / Theke** | Greek: *case, chest, receptacle* (τίθημι — to place). Not a “deck” as topic. |
| Unit poured into a Theke | **Dump** | English operational term for raw capture items. |

## Why not “Deck”

“Deck” implied cards/topic ontology. Chaos is unlimited dump; the container is a binder you pour into.

## Storage

Route/storage ids may still say `deck` / `deckId` for compatibility. UI and docs use **Theke** / **Dump**.
