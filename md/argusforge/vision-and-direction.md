## One sentence

ArgusForge exists so **formation** (judgment, methods, errors, context) can be captured in Chaos, related in Argus, timed by MTA, prepared in Vault — and coordinated without collapsing into a notes app or a frozen 3D world.

---

## What ArgusForge is / is not

| ArgusForge **is** | ArgusForge **is not** |
|-------------------|------------------------|
| A coordinating system for formation transfer | A clone of Notion / Obsidian |
| A place Chaos, Argus, MTA, Vault meet | Alexandria (Alexandria is FROZEN and separate) |
| Home for operational work + Argus for spatial experiment | “Everything is a graph” forced on every screen |
| A path toward confirmable relations and later space | Auto-ontology from usage heat |
| Personal / family first, extensible later | A single-AI chat wrapper |

## The fortuitous separation (keep it)

During AF03 UI work, two surfaces separated naturally. That split is now **product direction**:

```text
HOME + ACTIVE/ARCHIVE LISTS     =  traditional operational ArgusForge
ARGUS (Treemap → molecular)     =  experimental spatial / relational lab
```

| Layer | Job | Stability |
|-------|-----|-----------|
| **Home** | Overview, attention pulse, browse, recent decks | **Stable** — do not replace with Treemap |
| **Active / Archive lists** | Folder + deck administration (`RepositoryView`) | **Stable** — traditional management |
| **Argus** | Realm Treemap → open Realm → molecular Chaos Decks | **Experimental** — iterate freely |
| **Chaos Deck** | Fragments live here | Stable capture surface |
| **Vault** | Prepare formation packages for review | Early prototype |

**Rule:** Selecting Active or Archive from Home (or list links) opens the **traditional** repository views. Argus secondary Focus | Active | Archive filters the **Treemap** (same Realms, no copies) — that is Argus-lab language, not a second filing cabinet.

## Where we are going

### Near (Argus lab)

- Sharpen molecular language (mass ≠ raw fragment count; links ≠ affinity).  
- Let Focus become an attention filter without duplicating Realms.  
- Prepare optional fields for future MTA signals (`affinityScore`, decay, suggestions) — **display + confirm/reject**, no silent formal relations.  
- Keep lists for power users; do not force Treemap as the only management UI.

### Mid (engines)

- Argus Engine: stronger typed relations over Chaos source (still human-confirmable).  
- MTA Engine (boundary): recurrence, decay, use — **never** auto-become semantic edges.  
- Vault: richer formation packages without becoming Memory.

### Far (space — not now)

- Alexandria reopen only under a dedicated phase.  
- Possible mapping (non-binding until then):

```text
Realm      → spatial domain
Molecule   → possible Parcour / knowledge group
Chaos Deck → major station / body
Fragment   → material for loci / learning objects
```

Do **not** implement 3D, Parcour, or Alexandria integration from AF.
