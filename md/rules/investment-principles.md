# Investment principles

MATRIX v2 — applies beyond the H001–H030 experiment cycle.

## Core principles

1. Never overwrite historical data
2. Every closed trade is immutable
3. Every analysis is versioned
4. Every thesis can evolve while preserving history
5. Losses are more valuable than gains
6. **Capital preservation has priority**

## Trading rules

- Capital preservation first
- Never average down without a new thesis
- Every trade has a stop
- Every stop is respected
- Document everything
- Never delete history

## ChatGPT role

ChatGPT is the **reasoning engine** — already trained on your style and process.  
The repo and app provide **structure and state**, not a blank slate.

## Decision framework

Every new investment must answer:

- Why this company?
- Why now?
- Why not later?
- Biggest risks
- Worst-case scenario
- Expected upside
- Maximum acceptable loss
- Exit conditions

See `md/topics/decision-framework.md` and `md/topics/analysis-workflow.md`.
